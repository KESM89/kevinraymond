Good Friday
April 3, 2026

It's Good Friday. Another Friday to me, considering I'm an atheist.

Now that's the way to start a blog! Annoy some folks right off the rip.

That's not my intention; it's just what came to mind. And I'm hoping that's what this site will be. My space to say what I want. You, possible reader, don't have to like it.

The good news is there are plenty of websites, blogs, and social media accounts to fit your fancy.

When I was young, I was told, "If you don't like what you're watching, then turn the channel."

This is a personal space that I've chosen to make public. It may or may not be interesting to some. It's probably going to be boring, especially when I'm rambling about sports or stocks.

Also, this is an ongoing project. Claude AI helped me build the site because I'm not all that good at coding. I'm a sheet metal man by trade. A tin knocker who knows a thing or two about ductwork, HVAC and the trades.

So this is post number one, with hopefully many more to come. I just wanted to get the ball rolling.
